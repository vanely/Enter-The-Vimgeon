## Design axes

- **Ammo vs cooldown** — pressure vs rhythm.
- **Damage / pierce / AoE** — how dangerous on a small grid.
- **Projectile glyph** — must read clearly on the floor.
- **Unlock timing** — tutorial vs dungeon depth.
